Project Overview

This project is a school assignment that required me to design a revamped app and website homepage for a local brand in Singapore. I was required to first build a design system that includes all the UI elements necessary for the screens, stylescapes to communicate a certain look and feel, and more. Afterwards, I designed the app and website homepage according to the design system. *This project is for a school assignment so some functions/pages may not be working or completed.

Design System

This design system is made up of many components, rules, and style guides that aids in the design and development of the app and website. In this case, it aids me in designing the interfaces of the app and website.
