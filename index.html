My Projects

Birds of Paradise App and Website

I redesigned the mobile app and website for Birds of Paradise, a local gelato company in Singapore. The process included UX research on the existing app and website, creating a design system for UI elements, and crafting mood boards and style scapes to define the visual direction. Finally, I produced high-fidelity interactive prototypes for the app and website.

TimePiggy

I developed a mobile app prototype for a time bank, designed to facilitate users in grasping the concept of time banking. The process involved ideation through sketching, initial creation of low-fidelity wireframes, collecting user feedback, refining the wireframes into high-fidelity versions, and ultimately the development of an interactive prototype.

Mech

I developed a website for my startup concept using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The process included the creation of both low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes, ensuring the incorporation of essential content. Following this, I actively sought and incorporated user feedback. Lastly, I coded the website.
