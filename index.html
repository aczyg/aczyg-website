My Projects

Birds of Paradise App and Website

I designed a revamped mobile app and website prototype for Birds of Paradise, a local gelato company in Singapore.

The process entailed conducting UX research on the brand's current app and website, establishing a design system to identify the required UI elements for screen designs, developing mood boards and style scapes to convey a specific look and feel for the app and website. Lastly, I designed low-fidelity to high-fidelity wireframes to make the interactive prototypes. *This project is for a school assignment so some functions/pages may not be working or completed.

TimePiggy

I developed a mobile app prototype for a time bank, designed to facilitate users in grasping the concept of time banking. The process involved ideation through sketching, initial creation of low-fidelity wireframes, collecting user feedback, refining the wireframes into high-fidelity versions, and ultimately the development of an interactive prototype. *This project is for a school assignment so some functions/pages may not be working or completed.

Mech

I developed a website for my startup concept using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The process included the creation of both low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes, ensuring the incorporation of essential content. Following this, I actively sought and incorporated user feedback. Lastly, I coded the website. *This project is for a school assignment so some functions/pages may not be working or completed.
